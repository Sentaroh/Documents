Instructions for sending logs to developers

1. Start SMBSync3 and tap menu button.

2. Tap “Log management”

3. Tap “Enable logging”

4. Tap “OK” then tap “Back” button.

5. Perform sync and reproduce the error.

6. Tap “Log management” on menu then tap "Send to developer".

7. Tap “Send”

 

8. Describe the problem or question about SMBSync3 and tap "Send".

9. Select favorite E-Mail client.
